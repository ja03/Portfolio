01. About Me

Hello! My name is Ahmad Mahmoud, and I am a driven web developer with a passion for creating beautiful, user-friendly websites.

Although I'm not an expert in the field yet and I'm still learning JavaScript, my journey in web development started in 2022 when I discovered the exciting world of HTML, CSS, and JS. Since then, I have been continuously honing my skills and have become proficient in creating stunning websites that leave a lasting impression on visitors. Adding engaging animations and effects that captivate users and keep them coming back for more is something that I take pride in.

Recently, I have expanded my knowledge by delving into the realm of UI/UX design. I find great satisfaction in conducting research, performing interviews, and analyzing data to create designs that cater to the needs and preferences of users. By using my skills in web development and design, I am able to produce websites that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional.

Alongside my expertise in web development, I have also been working as a C++ teacher for over six months, where I have gained valuable experience in teaching and mentoring college students. Currently, I am in the process of writing a book that covers the basics of C++ programming.
